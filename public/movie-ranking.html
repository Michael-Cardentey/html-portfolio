The Best Movies According To Me

The top 3 movies of all time are:

1. Oldboy

This is my all time favorite movie, an engaging tale of revenge that draws you into the characters journey and motivations

2. Everything, Everywhere, All at Once

This movie is a heartwarming and chaotic story about a mother and her daughter

3. Fight Club

There is a reason this psychological thriller is considered a classic, the characters and action are all stellar
